About Me

Jupiter Westbard is a computer enthusiast with a passion for making music and movies. His software development experience includes machine learning, spatial and geometric algorithms, image processing, digital signal processing, and all sorts of creative tools. Jupiter has recently earned a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Tufts University, where he also minored in film production.

Now that he's done poring over problem sets, you may find him teaching math to grades K-12, implementing obscure algorithms, making songs or short films, bouldering, or enthusiastically patronizing Dunkin Donuts.
